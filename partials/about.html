About us

We are a community of artists, art enthusiasts, and people who just like looking at art. We seek to find matches for artists and art lovers.

We are currently in the pre-alpha stage of our journey, but we hope to release a full product in the near future

Some of the technologies we use are:

  • Angular2
  • Bootstrap
  • MongoDb
